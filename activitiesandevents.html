ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

Mallorca offers a vibrant array of activities and events, blending rich cultural experiences with outdoor adventures. Whether you're exploring its stunning landscapes, soaking up the lively atmosphere of local festivals, or enjoying world-class sports and entertainment, there's always something exciting to do. From its beautiful beaches to its historic towns, Mallorca provides endless opportunities for relaxation, exploration, and unforgettable moments year-round.

Big & upcoming

Mallorca Live Festival 2025

Mallorca Live Festival is an electrifying celebration of music, culture, and vibrant energy, set against the stunning backdrop of Mallorca’s beautiful coastline. With a lineup of top international artists, this festival promises unforgettable performances, beach vibes, and a one-of-a-kind Mediterranean experience. Don’t miss out on the ultimate summer adventure!
